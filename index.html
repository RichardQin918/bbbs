About Us

Baby by the Sea offers a different type of spa experience for babies and toddlers. The hydrotherapy service provides a floatation experience for youngsters that is second to none. Baby by the Sea is owned and operated by Dabao & Xiaobao Inc â€“ a corporation founded by 2 young professionals and new moms, Cecilia and Zoe. Before the birth of her son XiaoBao, Cecilia spent 5 years working for a large residential renovation company managing a project coordination team. Zoe has always held an entrepreneurial spirit from running a home-based salon to a flooring company both as a retailer and as a wholesaler and when her daughter Dabao was born Zoe could see the business opportunity available in the baby fitness industry. As mothers, their goal was to create a baby spa with a safe, clean and fun environment for both parents and babies. A special atmosphere was created inside the facility that promotes a special bonding experience between the babies and their parents and allows parents to connect with other families with children.

Benefit

Physical Health

Floatation has become an extremely popular form of hydrotherapy for babies and infants across Europe and Asia. Its benefits are undeniable and studies have shown that floatation as an exercise has the ability to reduce constipation and colic, bloating and gas in youngsters. As well, the physical activity provides a workout for the abdomen and legs. The young children love it because they get to do a number of fun activities and both parents and babies love the bonding opportunity that always comes with it.
